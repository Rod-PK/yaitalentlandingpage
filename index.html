GET TO KNOW THE INNOVATOR

YAPOA BABA,

the visionary founder of Blessing Weaves, is a trailblazing entrepreneur from Drobonso Zongo, Ashanti Region, dedicated to revolutionizing the Kente and smock weaving industry. With a deep passion for cultural preservation and women's economic empowerment, she has redefined traditional weaving by making it more accessible and inclusive.

Recognizing the physical challenges that conventional weaving machines pose for women, she collaborated with local artisans to redesign and simplify these tools, enabling more women to master the craft with ease. Through her leadership, Blessing Weaves has successfully trained 23 women in Kente and smock weaving.

Committed to expanding opportunities in the Clothing & Inclusion Support for Women sector, she envisions scaling training programs, empowering more women, and fostering a sustainable future where Ghana's rich textile heritage continues to thrive.
